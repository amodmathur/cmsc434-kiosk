Welcome to the Brendan Iribe Center Directory page! This is where you will find a floorplan for every single floor located within the Iribe Center. For now these pages are PDF files, which allow you to scroll around and zoom in on certain areas that you want yo look at. In the future, I plan on converting these PDF's to images, utilizing a website that I found called 'image-map.net' that will allow me to make these images interactable. This will allow the user to click on specific rooms to see professor offices, etc.
