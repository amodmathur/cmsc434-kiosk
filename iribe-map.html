Brendan Iribe Center - Building Directory

A hub for technology, collaboration, and discovery at the heart of a new innovation district, the Brendan Iribe Center for Computer Science and Engineering embodies UMD's excellence in both of these fields while also providing a space for students, faculty, and staff alike to contribute and be part of the future. Open work areas, makerspaces, and flexible classrooms (including our 100-seat and 300-seat auditoriums) promote teamwork and active learning. Outside of the basement and the sixth floor, students are welcome to utilize conference rooms, common study areas, classrooms, as well as visit faculty offices (visit tab) in order to complete all of their work.
